Public exposures

Posted onOctober 19, 2020

Interior Health updates this list with the locations and times of known possible exposures to COVID-19 to the public in our region.

Public Health asks anyone who may have visited the locations listed below to follow the direction contained in the announcement. If you develop any symptoms of COVID-19, please seek testing and then self-isolate.

If you have been identified as a COVID-19 case or close contact, Public Health will contact you directly to provide further instruction.
